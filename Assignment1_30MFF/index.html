30MFF Film

Description

This is a 30MFF shot by 3 students from the class: Communications Lab (IM-UH 1011) at New York University, Abu Dhabi. The task was to finish shooting a video and edit it within 30 minutes. The video must have a story, a meaning, and should make sense (something that is not totally random). Our team came up with this idea as it is common for university students and we used our soft toys (featured as Coco and Ostap) to depict what we wanted to convey.
